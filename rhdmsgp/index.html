ECU Tune to Safely and Effectively Maximize the Performance of Your Car

Every vehicle comes with an Engine Control Unit (ECU) out of factory, a small electronic box that monitors and optimizes the operation and performance of your car per manufacturer's specifications. The factory sometimes makes numerous compromises in their design to balance investment and quality that may lead to limiting the vehicle's potential and fine tuning may not be cost effective for their purpose.
This is where a high performance tuning comes in to remap the ECU from the factory tune to bring out the vehicle's full potential, performance as well as safety. There are various parameters and components involved in this process and it is critical to work with a highly experienced and capable protuner like Bains Tuning to effectively and safely achieve what your vehicle is capable of.

Bains Tuning is the only COBB certified protuner in San Francisco Bay Area

Bains Tuning specializes in Subaru, Evo, GT-R and Honda via platforms like COBB, Ecutek, Hondata and KTuner. Your vehicle is tuned to use various fuel types including regular pump gasoline, E85, and Flex Fuel, depending on your mods and goals that are carefully discussed with your tuner.

Flexible Location Options Available

We offer following three types locations for tuning: Our dyno is conveniently located in San Jose, CA for those from the bay area/NorCal region. While dyno tuning is the most consistent way to tune, we also offer street tuning that is performed near a location that is convenient for you. Lastly, we tune cars remotely, aka E-tuning. We have tuned clients' cars from all over the continent including Alaska, east coast and Canada.
